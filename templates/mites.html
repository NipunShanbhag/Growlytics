Mites Information

Mites are tiny arachnids that cause significant crop damage by sucking plant juices, leading to yellowing, bronzing, and leaf drop.

Damage Type:
Sucking damage
Favorable Conditions:
Hot, dry weather
Detection:
Fine webbing, stippling
Severity:
Very High

Mite Control Strategy

1
Early Detection:
Monitor undersides of leaves for stippling and webbing
2
Application Timing:
Spray during cooler parts of day (early morning/evening)
3
Coverage:
Ensure thorough coverage of leaf undersides where mites feed
4
Rotation:
Rotate between different mode of action products

Safety & Application Guidelines

  • Wear protective equipment including respirator and gloves
  • Apply when wind speed is low to prevent drift
  • Maintain proper water pH (6.0-7.0) for optimal efficacy
  • Do not exceed recommended dosage to prevent phytotoxicity
  • Follow pre-harvest interval strictly
  • Store products in cool, dry place away from children
